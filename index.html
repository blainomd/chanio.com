The patterns chanio finds

Your browsing tells a health story
you have never read.

Most health-related browsing is research without action — you circle a topic, run the same searches, and never take the next step. chanio finds the signal in the noise.

"You've searched hip pain management 18 times this month. You visited two orthopedic surgeon sites but never called. SurgeonValue can show you what a consult actually costs — before you pick up the phone."
Gradual shifts in health searches reveal clinical patterns that unfold too slowly to notice in real time.
"You have browsed home care agencies in Boulder 11 times. You have never found a pricing page. co-op.care shows you $25/hr W-2 caregivers — no agency markup, no turnover."
Care-seeking research without conversion usually means pricing opacity. chanio connects intent to the right starting point.
"You have read three articles about HSA eligible expenses in the last week. You are leaving $936 in average annual tax savings unclaimed. A physician LMN takes 48 hours."
Financial health research without an action path is the most common missed opportunity chanio surfaces.
Privacy by design

Your data stays on your device.

chanio processes locally. No server sees your browsing history. No cloud storage. No data sold to advertisers. This is the opposite of how the internet works today. Platforms build profiles of you to sell. chanio builds a profile of you to give back to you.

Local processing only No cloud storage No data selling You own the profile Uninstall = data gone
