Your searches tell a story you've never read.

chanio is an AI companion that watches how you browse -- with your permission -- and surfaces the patterns, interests, and obsessions you didn't know you had.

How it works

1

Connect your browser

Install the chanio extension. It takes 30 seconds. Works in Chrome, Arc, Brave, and Edge.

2

chanio watches passively

No interruptions. No pop-ups. It quietly observes your browsing patterns in the background.

3

Weekly insight report

"Here's what your internet says about you." Patterns you would never see yourself, delivered once a week.

4

Your interest graph grows

Over time, chanio builds a map of the real you -- not the algorithmic you that platforms sell to advertisers.

What chanio sees that you don't

Your browsing history contains signal buried in noise. chanio finds the signal.

"You've researched mushroom cultivation 14 times this month but never bought anything. Ready to start?"

chanio detects recurring interest loops -- topics you orbit without committing to -- and nudges you toward action.

"Your health searches shifted from back pain to hip pain over 6 months. Worth mentioning to your doctor?"

Gradual shifts in search topics reveal patterns that unfold too slowly for you to notice in real time.

"You've looked at 23 properties in Boulder under $600K. Should we set up alerts?"

When your browsing signals intent, chanio can connect you to tools that accelerate the next step.

Your data stays on your device.

chanio processes locally. No server, no cloud, no selling your data. You own your attention. The interest graph lives on your machine, not ours.

This is the opposite of how the internet works today. Platforms build profiles of you to sell to advertisers. chanio builds a profile of you to give back to you.

Local processing No cloud storage No data selling You own everything
