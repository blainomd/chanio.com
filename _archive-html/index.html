What chanio does

Browse. Remember. Know.

How it works

Installed in seconds. Working in the background.

1

Install the extension

Add chanio to Chrome. One click. No account required to start. The ticker appears at the bottom of every page you visit.

2

Browse normally

chanio runs quietly in the background. As you read, it summarizes the page — topic, key points, your time on it. You see nothing until you want to.

3

Memory builds automatically

Summaries accumulate into a browsing profile, organized by channel. Health reads in one bucket. Music in another. Finance in another.

4

Feed it to your AI

Connect chanio to Claude, ChatGPT, or any AI that accepts context. Your browsing memory becomes the AI's working memory — so it already knows what you know.

Privacy by design

Your memory. Not ours.

chanio does not sell your browsing history. It does not build a profile to target ads. Your data lives with you and goes only where you choose to send it.
